Why Is This Page Created?


  • Back to main page

    Why was this page created? The YouTube community—who doesn't know that place?
    Your mind is forced to adhere to community guidelines.
    Guidelines that, while not entirely wrong, often silence criticism of tyrannical rulers, especially when expressed informally.

    How long will this anger be suppressed?
    How long must we obey?
    How long will we continue to be battered by community guidelines?
    How long will we continue to be crushed by the regime's sycophantic tankies?

    Finally, we decided to reclaim control of our own voices—by creating this page.
    Here, we are free to express our thoughts and opinions without restraint from anyone. Without authority, without masters, without tyrants.

    This is Rakyat Merdeka.

    Please note: most of the writing on this site is amateur. If there are any inconsistent, incorrect, or disrespectful words, we sincerely apologize.

    Long live the insurrection! Long live freedom!
